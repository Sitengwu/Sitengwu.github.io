Solar Installation

10kWh PV installation on a farm

During the 2024 June/July holidays, I was given the opportunity to install a 10kWh PV power system at a farmhouse in Doornkop, KZN. The house was being renovated after being neglected for 15 years. The project took two weeks and required diligent planning, utilizing many of the skills I've acquired during my university career. Furthermore, this project was undertaken entirely on my own. The total installation included six 550W solar panels, two 5kWh 110Ah batteries, and a 5.5kW inverter. Lights and plugs, as well as the main house distribution board (DB), were also installed since the previous electrical systems were destroyed. A Certificate of Compliance (COC) was also acquired after the installation.

  • Year: 2024
  • Location: Doornkop, KZN

Audio Localisation

Real-time tracking of a sound source

In 2023, for the Electrical and Computer Engineering design course EEE3097S, two other teammates and I designed an audio localization platform using MEMS microphones and a Raspberry Pi Zero W. Our team built a system that can detect the position of a speaker playing any sound, even loud, blaring music, in an 800x500mm grid space in real-time. It was an amazing experience, and we had many memorable moments. Here's a link to our project GitHub repo.

  • Year: 2023
  • Location: UCT, WhiteLab

Industrial Surveilance System

IP camera system for a clothing factory.

I was given the opportunity to install a brand new surveillance system for a large industrial clothing factory in Isithebe Industrial Park. As my first major project, it marked the start of my journey as an engineer. After having just matriculated, I worked for two months during December 2020 and January 2021. I installed a dual NVR, 32-camera Hikvision IP surveillance system, which is still running today. It has proven very useful over the last few years, and I return during holidays to perform maintenance and repairs.

  • Year: 2020
  • Location: Isithebe, Mandeni, KZN
